The Ecosystem

One loop. Complete health.

Three intelligent components working in harmony — tracking, thinking, and feeding — so your pet gets exactly what they need, when they need it.

Smart Brooch

A lightweight wearable that tracks heart rate, temperature, activity levels, and behavioral patterns in real time.

AI Health Engine

Our proprietary algorithm calculates metabolic energy expenditure and converts it into precise nutritional requirements.

Smart Feeder

Dispenses the exact portion of food your pet needs — no overfeeding, no guesswork. UV-C disinfected for hygiene.

The Tech Core

The Metabolic Feedback Loop

A self-correcting cycle that continuously adapts to your pet's real-time health data.

01

Track

The Smart Brooch continuously monitors heart rate, body temperature, and activity levels throughout the day.

Calculate

AI analyzes the biometric data to calculate real-time energy expenditure and nutritional needs using metabolic modeling.
